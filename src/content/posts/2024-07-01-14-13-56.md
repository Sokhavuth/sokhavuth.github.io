This film tells the story of a little girl, An Qi, who is persistently looking for a coin that fell into the sewer to clarify the truth of the young man Sun Zhiming being framed, so as to reveal the influence of the greed and hypocrisy of human nature in social life on the next generation. It will play a positive role in helping primary and middle school students form a correct outlook on life, the world, and values.
