The film is a road parent-child comedy. Qin Yong plays a rock singer Heimeng. At the peak of his career, he married the movie star Wang Xinyu and had a daughter Doudou. Originally, his life could be happy forever, but because of his addiction to music and his personality The eccentricity made his wife leave him with their daughter. After the divorce, Heimeng fell into the trough of his life after being blocked and terminated by the company. With the help of his agent and daughter, he started a self-driving tour in Yunnan. In the end, he returned to the stage to create miracles and harvest family, love and friendship.
