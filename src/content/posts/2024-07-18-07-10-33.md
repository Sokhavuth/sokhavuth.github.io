The radio host Zhuo Zhentao (played by Dai Liren) is very talented, and the emotional psychology program he hosts is deeply loved by the audience. He also has an enviable family, his wife (played by Liang Jing) is a virtuous housekeeper, his eldest daughter Xiaoyi is a rising star on the runway, his son Xiaobai (played by Zhai Wenbin) is a famous stylist, and his youngest daughter Xiaoqian is fresh and lovely , With the unfortunate murder of his wife in the aquarium, this happy family has undergone tremendous changes. The police confirmed that it was homicide, and Zhuo Zhentao was burdened with a great psychological burden because he was busy with work and neglected his family. The three children were born to two wives, both of whom died. Xiaoyi puts the responsibility on her father. Although the half-children get along well, Zhuo Zhentao, who is a psychologist, is really hard to ignore. With yet another bizarre incident, the truth is about to emerge..
